Attaching Bodycam

In Uniform

/me Takes out bodycam, attaches it to chest, checks its ballistic and waterproof
/me ensures it is recording and checks for the red light
/do It is recording, is ballistic and waterproof
/me connects the PDA to the nearby SAHP cell tower and cloud servers

While Undercover

/me takes out bodycam and attaches it to belt, hides it, checks its ballistic and waterproof
/me makes sure it is recording and checks for the red light
/do It is recording, is ballistic and waterproof
/me connects PDA to the nearest cell tower
🔄

Refreshing Bodycam

Refreshing Bodycam

/me refreshing bodycam
/do It is recording

⚡💀 First, turn on the bodycam (or screen recording software), then type the command while making sure you are clearly visible in the recording as you do it.

💾

Saving Bodycam

Saving Bodycam

/me saves bodycam footage, uploads it to SAHP servers, and continues recording
/do It is recording

Breaking Trunk of Vehicle

Breaking the Trunk

/me Break the trunk wide open using a crowbar
/me writes down license plate and searches the license plate through the SAHP database
/me Kneels down, feels the VIN number under the car and run it through SAHP Database

Handing Footage to the Lawyer

Finishing Your Shift
